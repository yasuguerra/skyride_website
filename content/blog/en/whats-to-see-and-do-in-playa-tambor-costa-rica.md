## Playa Tambor: Costa Rica's hidden beach paradise

Playa Tambor is one of Costa Rica's most peaceful coastal destinations. Located on the Nicoya Peninsula, it offers miles of untouched beach, gentle waves, and a welcoming local community — without the crowds of Guanacaste or Manuel Antonio.

## Things to do in Playa Tambor

- **Surf** — Gentle waves perfect for beginners
- **Snorkel** — Reefs at Tortuga Island (20 min by boat)
- **Turtle watching** — Montezuma and Cabuya beaches (July–December season)
- **Hiking** — Cabo Blanco Nature Reserve, Costa Rica's first national park
- **Horseback riding** — Sunset rides along the beach
- **Yoga & wellness** — Numerous retreats in the area

## How to get there from Panama

From Panama City, Playa Tambor is just **1 hour 30 minutes by private flight**. The commercial alternative involves flying to San José + 4-hour bus + ferry — over 10 hours total.

## Where to stay

- **Tango Mar** — Boutique resort with an oceanfront golf course
- **Tambor Tropical** — Suites with private pools
- **Local cabañas** — More affordable and authentic options in town

## Best time to visit

December through April (dry season). Rains from May to November are intense but brief, and prices drop significantly.
