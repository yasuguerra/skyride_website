## David, Chiriquí: Panama's Other Side

Most private flight passengers in Panama discover San Blas, Contadora, or Bocas del Toro. Few find David — the capital of Chiriquí province. That makes it one of the country's best-kept secrets.

David is more than a city. It's the **gateway to the Chiriquí Highlands**: Barú Volcano (Panama's highest peak), the mountain town of Boquete, highland coffee farms, and the best year-round climate in the country. And it's just **55 minutes by private flight** from Panama City.

## What to Do in David and the Chiriquí Highlands

### Boquete: The Perfect Valley
35 km from David, Boquete is Panama's most charming mountain town. Average temperature of 19°C, coffee farms on the slopes of Barú, whitewater rafting on the Chiriquí River, and famous hiking up to the volcano. Coffee lovers shouldn't miss the Geisha coffee farm tours — Geisha is the most expensive coffee in the world, and it's grown here.

**Activities in Boquete:**
- Hiking to Barú Volcano summit (Panama's highest point, 3,475 m)
- Coffee tours at historic farms (Finca Lerida, Kotowa, La Esmeralda)
- Grade 3–4 whitewater rafting on the Chiriquí River
- Bird watching (400+ species in the region)
- Zip-lining through cloud forest

### Barú Volcano
The only mountain in the world where you can see both the Pacific Ocean and the Caribbean Sea at the same time on a clear day. The pre-dawn hike to watch the sunrise from the summit is one of Panama's most iconic experiences.

### Playa La Barqueta
30 km from David, this wide dark-sand Pacific beach is ideal for surfers and nature lovers. Sea turtle nesting zone from May to November.

### Guadalupe and Cerro Punta
At higher altitude than Boquete, these villages on Barú's slopes are known for flower gardens, strawberry farms, and the most pristine nature in the region. Average temperature of 13–16°C.

## Travel Tips for David and Chiriquí

- **Best season:** December to May (dry season in Chiriquí). The highlands have cool weather year-round.
- **Pack:** Bring a jacket. Boquete can drop to 15°C at night.
- **Accommodation:** For nature, look for lodges outside downtown Boquete. For business, David's center has international hotel chains.
- **Getting around:** Rent a 4x4 in David to comfortably reach Boquete and Cerro Punta.
