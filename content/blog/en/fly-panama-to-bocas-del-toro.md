## Bocas del Toro: Panama's Caribbean by air

Bocas del Toro is synonymous with beach, surf, reggae, and wild nature. But getting there by land from Panama City can take **10+ hours by bus or 6 hours by car**. On a private flight, you land in 1 hour.

## Why fly to Bocas?

- **Time savings** — 1 hour vs. 6–10 hours by land
- **Comfort** — No mountain roads or tight curves
- **Direct arrival** — Landing at Isla Colón airport
- **Spectacular views** — Flyover of the Darién and Caribbean coastline

## What to do in Bocas del Toro

- Starfish Beach (Playa de las Estrellas)
- Surfing at Playa Bluff and Paunch
- Snorkeling at Cayo Zapatilla
- Overwater restaurants on Isla Colón
- Mangrove boat tour
- Dolphin watching in Dolphin Bay

## Travel tips

- Bocas is a rainy area — pack a light raincoat
- The best season is September–October and February–March
- Bring cash for the smaller islands
- Book overwater accommodation for the full experience
