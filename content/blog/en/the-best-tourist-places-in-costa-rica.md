## The best tourist destinations in Costa Rica

Costa Rica packs an incredible density of natural wonders into a small country. Active volcanoes, cloud forests, two oceans, and biodiversity representing 5% of the world's species.

## Top 10 destinations

### 1. Arenal Volcano

Costa Rica's most photogenic volcano. Natural hot springs, hiking trails, and breathtaking views. Private flight from Panama: 2 hours.

### 2. Monteverde

Cloud forest with hanging bridges, zip lines, and over 400 bird species. An experience that exists nowhere else on Earth.

### 3. Manuel Antonio

A national park where the jungle meets the beach. Monkeys, sloths, iguanas, and postcard-perfect beaches in one compact area.

### 4. Playa Tambor

Absolute tranquility on the Nicoya Peninsula. Perfect for those seeking beach without crowds.

### 5. Tortuguero

The canals of Costa Rica's Caribbean coast. Accessible only by boat or plane, it's the most important nesting site for green sea turtles.

### 6. Corcovado

National Geographic called it "the most biologically intense place on Earth." Primary rainforest, tapirs, jaguars, and scarlet macaws.

### 7. Guanacaste

Surf beaches, all-inclusive resorts, and the most consistent sunshine in Costa Rica. Tamarindo, Flamingo, and Conchal are the highlights.

### 8. Cocos Island

A UNESCO World Heritage Site 550 km offshore. Only accessible by 36-hour marine expedition. World-class diving with hammerhead sharks.

### 9. Puerto Viejo

Caribbean Costa Rica with reggae vibes, Afro-Caribbean cuisine, and wild beaches. Punta Uva is one of the prettiest in the country.

### 10. Rincón de la Vieja

An active volcano with fumaroles, mud pools, and waterfalls. Less touristy than Arenal but equally impressive.
