## Apps for finding private airplane flights

Private aviation has modernized. You no longer need to call a broker or have exclusive contacts — there are platforms and apps that let you quote and book private flights from your phone.

## The best options in Latin America

### Sky Ride (direct WhatsApp)

For flights in Panama, Costa Rica, and Colombia, Sky Ride offers instant quotes via WhatsApp. No middlemen, no hidden fees. Response in under 10 minutes.

### Avinode

The world's largest platform for aviation brokers. Not direct-to-consumer, but many operators use it to publish availability.

### PrivateFly

A global app with online quoting. Good for long international routes, fewer options for short Central American routes.

### BLADE

Popular in the United States for helicopters and jets. Expanding to new markets but with limited presence in Latin America.

### JetSmarter (now XO)

Membership + marketplace for shared flights and charters. Focused on the United States and Europe.

## Which one to choose for Panama?

For flights within Panama and to Costa Rica/Colombia, **direct contact with the local operator** remains the most efficient option. Global platforms add intermediaries and commissions.

Sky Ride operates its own fleet, which allows:
- Direct prices without intermediary markup
- Total schedule flexibility
- Personalized coordination (special luggage, pets, etc.)

## The future of private aviation

Flight-sharing apps (like Sky Ride's available seats) are democratizing access. It's only a matter of time before booking a private flight is as simple as ordering an Uber.
