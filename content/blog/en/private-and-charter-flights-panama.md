## Private and charter flights in Panama

Private aviation in Panama has grown significantly in recent years. It's no longer just for millionaire entrepreneurs — families, groups of friends, and couples are discovering that **sharing a private flight can be as affordable as a commercial flight** when split among several passengers.

## Types of private flights

### Exclusive charter flight

You rent the entire aircraft. You choose the time, destination, and travel only with your group. It's the option with the most flexibility and privacy.

### Available seats (shared flights)

Sky Ride offers individual seats on scheduled flights to popular destinations like Contadora, San Blas, and Bocas del Toro. You pay only for your seat, not the entire aircraft.

### Executive transfer

Point-to-point flights for executives with tight schedules. Ideal for meetings in the interior of the country or business trips to Costa Rica and Colombia.
