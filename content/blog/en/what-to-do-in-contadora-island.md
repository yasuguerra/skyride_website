## Why fly private to Contadora?

Contadora Island is the jewel of the Pearl Islands archipelago and the favorite getaway for those looking for a quick escape from Panama City. In **just 20 minutes of flight**, you leave the urban traffic behind and land in a paradise of white sand and turquoise waters.

## The best beaches on Contadora

Contadora has more than 10 beaches, each with its own personality. These are the must-visits:

- **Playa Larga** — The longest and most peaceful. Ideal for sunset walks.
- **Playa Cacique** — Perfect for snorkeling thanks to its nearby reef.
- **Playa Galeón** — Popular with families for its calm waters.
- **Playa Ejecutiva** — Private access and premium services.

## What to do during your visit

Beyond the beaches, Contadora offers experiences for every taste:

- Snorkeling and diving at pristine reefs
- Whale watching (season July-October)
- Kayak tours to neighboring islands
- Caribbean cuisine at oceanfront restaurants
