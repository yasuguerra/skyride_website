## Transportation to Contadora Island: all your options

Contadora is the most accessible island in the Pearl Islands archipelago, located 80 km from Panama City in the Pacific Ocean. Here we evaluate every transportation option available.

## 1. Private flight (the fastest)

- **Time**: 20 minutes
- **From**: Marcos A. Gelabert Airport (Albrook)
- **Price**: From $950 for up to 5 passengers
- **Advantage**: Total schedule flexibility, no seasickness, spectacular aerial views

With Sky Ride you can coordinate departure at your convenience. Available aircraft: Cessna 206, Piper Azteca, Cessna Grand Caravan (up to 12 pax).

## 2. Ferry (the most affordable)

- **Time**: 2–2.5 hours
- **From**: Amador Causeway
- **Price**: $55–85 USD per person
- **Disadvantage**: Fixed schedules, frequent cancellations, seasickness in open water

## 3. Private boat

- **Time**: 1.5–2 hours
- **From**: Various docks in Panama
- **Price**: $500–1,200 depending on the vessel
- **Disadvantage**: Weather-dependent, can be uncomfortable with waves

## 4. Organized tour

Some tour operators offer full-day packages including transportation (usually ferry) + lunch + activities.

- **Price**: $120–200 per person
- **Disadvantage**: No flexibility, group schedule

## Quick comparison

| Option | Time | Price/person (5 pax) | Flexibility |
|--------|------|---------------------|------------|
| Private flight | 20 min | ~$190 | Total |
| Ferry | 2.5 h | $55–85 | Low |
| Private boat | 2 h | ~$150 | Medium |
| Tour | Full day | $120–200 | None |

## Which should you choose?

For weekend getaways or business trips, **private flights** maximize your time on the island. For tight budgets and flexible travelers, the ferry works well during dry season.
