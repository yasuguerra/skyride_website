## Long Beach on Contadora Island

Long Beach (Playa Larga) is, as its name suggests, the largest beach on Contadora Island. It stretches for nearly 700 meters of golden sand, with calm waters and panoramic views toward the neighboring islands of the Pearl Islands archipelago.

## Why choose Long Beach?

- **Privacy** — Unlike Executive Beach, Long Beach is rarely crowded
- **Sunsets** — The westward orientation offers the best sunsets on the island
- **Walking** — The beach's length makes it perfect for barefoot strolls
- **Families** — Shallow waters with no strong currents

## How to get there

From Contadora's airstrip, Long Beach is a 10-minute walk. No vehicle needed — the entire island can be explored on foot in under an hour.

## Nearby amenities

While the beach itself doesn't have commercial services, a few minutes away you'll find:

- Oceanfront restaurants (fresh seafood, ceviche, coconut rice)
- Kayak and snorkel equipment rentals
- Small boutique hotels and vacation rentals
