## How to fly private without overspending

Many people assume private flights are only for millionaires. The reality is different: when you split the cost among 3 to 5 passengers, **a charter flight can cost the same as a commercial first-class ticket**.

## Strategies to save

### 1. Share the flight

Sky Ride offers **available seats** on popular routes. You pay only for your seat, not the entire aircraft. Prices from $190/person to Contadora.

### 2. Travel in a group

A Cessna 206 for 5 passengers to Contadora costs $950. Split: $190/person. A group of 12 in a Grand Caravan: even more economical per person.

### 3. Book in low season

May through November has less demand. Prices can be up to 20% lower and aircraft availability is higher.

### 4. Be flexible with your schedule

Early morning flights (before 7 AM) or late afternoon (after 4 PM) may have discounts due to lower demand.

### 5. Repositioning flights

When an aircraft returns empty from a destination (ferry flight), Sky Ride offers those flights at a reduced price. Ask about availability.
