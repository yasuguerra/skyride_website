## Ferry to Contadora: is it really worth it?

If you're planning a trip to Contadora Island, you're probably wondering about the best way to get there. The ferry is the most well-known option, but is it the most convenient? Let's compare.

## Ferry vs. private flight comparison

| Factor | Ferry | Private flight |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Travel time | 2–2.5 hours | 20 minutes |
| Price (per person) | $55–85 USD | From $190 (5 pax) |
| Schedule | 1–2 departures/day | Your schedule |
| Seasickness | Common in open water | Not applicable |
| Luggage | Limited | 10–15 kg/person |
| Cancellations | Frequent due to weather | Rare |

## The problem with the ferry

The Contadora ferry departs from the Causeway (Amador) on fixed schedules: typically one early morning departure and one afternoon return. This means:

- **Half a day lost** just on transportation
- **Rough seas are common** — many passengers get seasick, especially May through November
- **Cancellations without notice** due to weather conditions
- **No flexibility** — if you miss the ferry, there's no plan B until the next day

## The alternative: private flight with Sky Ride

- Depart at the time you choose
- 20 minutes door to door
- Up to 12 passengers per flight
- No dependence on sea conditions
- Air-conditioned aircraft
