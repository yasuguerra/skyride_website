## A helicopter tour over Panama City

Imagine flying over the Panama City skyline, seeing the Canal from the air, and gazing at the Pacific islands from a perspective few people ever experience. A helicopter tour is the most exclusive experience you can have in the city.

## What do you see from the helicopter?

The typical 25-minute tour covers:

- **Casco Antiguo** — The colonial UNESCO World Heritage city seen from above
- **Punta Pacífica skyline** — The tallest skyscrapers in Central America
- **Panama Canal** — The Miraflores Locks and the Bridge of the Americas
- **Amador Causeway** — The connection between the city and the islands
- **Pacific coast** — Beaches, mangroves, and the Pearl Islands archipelago on the horizon

## The experience step by step

1. Arrive at the Albrook heliport (15 min before)
2. Safety briefing with your pilot
3. Takeoff — the first seconds are the most thrilling
4. Panoramic flight with pilot commentary
5. Landing and photo/video handoff

## Who is it for?

- **Couples** looking for a romantic experience
- **Tourists** who want to see everything in a short time
- **Executives** impressing international clients
- **Families** celebrating special occasions
- **Photographers** capturing unique aerial shots

## Tips for your flight

- Wear dark clothing to avoid reflections in photos
- Secure your phone or camera well
- If you suffer from vertigo, tell the pilot — they'll adjust the altitude
- Sunset flights are the most photogenic (book ahead)
