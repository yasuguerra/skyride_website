## Honeymoon in Costa Rica: the complete guide

Costa Rica combines adventure and romance like few destinations can. Volcanoes with natural hot springs, deserted Pacific beaches, jungles with secret waterfalls, and a "pura vida" culture that makes everything flow.

## Top 5 honeymoon destinations

### Arenal (La Fortuna)

Natural hot springs surrounded by rainforest, romantic dinners with volcano views, and adventures like waterfall rappelling and hanging bridges.

### Manuel Antonio

Beach + jungle in one place. Boutique hotels overlooking the ocean, capuchin monkeys as neighbors, and sunsets you'll remember forever.

### Monteverde

For adventurous couples: zip lines over the cloud forest, walks among orchids, and high-altitude coffee. Cool and romantic.

### Santa Teresa

Surf, yoga, and bohemian vibes on the Nicoya Peninsula. Open-air restaurants, endless beach, and a relaxed atmosphere perfect for newlyweds.

### Drake Bay (Osa)

Costa Rica at its wildest. Whale watching, diving at Caño Island, and eco-luxury lodges in the jungle. For couples seeking something different.

## Suggested itinerary (8 days)

- **Day 1**: Arrive in San José by private flight from Panama
- **Days 2–3**: Arenal — hot springs, volcano, waterfalls
- **Days 4–5**: Manuel Antonio — beach, national park, spa
- **Days 6–7**: Santa Teresa — surf, yoga, sunsets
- **Day 8**: Return to Panama
