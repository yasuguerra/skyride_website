## Everything about private helicopters in Panama

Flying in a private helicopter is no longer a luxury reserved for movies. In Panama, more and more people are choosing this form of transport for panoramic tours, executive transfers, and island getaways.

## When does it make sense to rent a helicopter?

- **Panoramic tours** over Panama City and the Canal
- **Quick transfers** to the interior of the country (Contadora, San Blas, Bocas)
- **Corporate events** — premium arrival for executives
- **Marriage proposals** and special celebrations
- **Professional aerial photography and filming**

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I fly with the doors open?**
Yes, it's a popular option on panoramic flights. Additional safety harnesses are provided.

**Is there a weight limit?**
Each aircraft has a maximum payload. We inform you of the limit when quoting based on the model.

**Can you take off from my hotel?**
It depends on the space. Some hotels have a helipad. Check availability when booking.
