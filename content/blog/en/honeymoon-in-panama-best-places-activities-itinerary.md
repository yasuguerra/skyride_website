## Honeymoon in Panama: best destinations by private flight

Panama is one of the world's most underrated honeymoon destinations. Paradise islands, tropical rainforest, a cosmopolitan city, and everything connected by private flights under one hour.

## Best honeymoon destinations

### Contadora Island (Pearl Islands)

20 minutes by air. White sand beaches, boutique hotels with ocean views, and romantic dinners facing the Pacific. The most accessible and exclusive destination.

### San Blas (Guna Yala)

30 minutes by air. Uninhabited islands with overwater cabins. The most authentic and disconnected experience — no WiFi, no stress, just sea and sky.

### Bocas del Toro

1 hour by air. Caribbean vibes, surfing, overwater restaurants, and relaxed nightlife. Ideal for adventurous couples.

### Boquete (Chiriquí)

45 minutes by air. Mountains, world-class coffee, hot springs, and hiking on Barú Volcano. Perfect for couples who prefer mountains over beaches.

## Suggested itinerary (7 days)

- **Days 1–2**: Panama City — Casco Viejo, rooftop bars, gastronomy
- **Days 3–4**: Fly to Contadora — beach, snorkel, romantic dinner
- **Days 5–6**: Fly to San Blas — total disconnection, kayaking, stargazing
- **Day 7**: Return to Panama City
