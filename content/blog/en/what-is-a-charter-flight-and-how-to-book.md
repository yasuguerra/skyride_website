## What is a charter flight?

A charter flight is a private flight where you rent the entire aircraft instead of buying a seat on a commercial airline. You choose the schedule, the destination, and travel only with your group.

## Advantages over commercial flights

- **No lines or check-in** — Arrive 15 minutes before takeoff
- **Flexible schedules** — You decide when to fly
- **Access to private airstrips** — Destinations airlines don't cover
- **Total privacy** — Ideal for executives and celebrities
- **Reduced flight time** — Direct routes with no layovers

## How much does a charter flight cost?

Prices depend on three factors: destination, aircraft type, and number of passengers.

- **Panama → Bocas del Toro** — From $2,200 (1 h)
- **Panama → Costa Rica** — From $3,400 (1 h 45 min)
- **Panama → Medellín** — From $5,500 (2 h)

## How to book a charter flight

The process with Sky Ride takes less than 10 minutes:

- Contact us via WhatsApp or phone call
- Share your date, origin, destination, and number of passengers
- Receive a personalized quote with aircraft options
- Confirm with credit card, bank transfer, or Wompi
- Receive your itinerary with check-in time and pilot details

## Required documentation

- Valid ID or passport (all passengers)
- For international flights: passport with at least 6 months validity
- Minors: authorization from both parents if traveling without them

## How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least **48 hours in advance**, although we can arrange flights within 24 hours depending on availability. For peak dates (December, Carnival, Easter), booking 2–3 weeks ahead guarantees your preferred aircraft.
