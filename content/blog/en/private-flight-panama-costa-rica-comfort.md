## Advantages of a private flight

- **No layovers** — direct point-to-point flight
- **Flexible schedule** — you decide the departure time
- **Simplified documentation** — streamlined immigration at the private terminal
- **Generous luggage** — 15 kg per passenger without strict restrictions
- **Premium arrival** — landing at airstrips close to your final destination

## Aircraft for this route

For international flights to Costa Rica we recommend:

- **King Air F90** — 6 passengers, twin turboprop, pressurized cabin
- **King Air 200** — 9 passengers, ideal for groups or families
- **Cessna Grand Caravan** — 12 passengers, the most economical option per person

## Required documentation

- Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
- For Panamanians: no visa required for Costa Rica
- Minors: notarized authorization if traveling without both parents
- Flight plan filed by Sky Ride with authorities in both countries
